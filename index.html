Reviews

The Strange Case of Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde

One of the best short novella I've read. Love the classic horror style it personifies, by starting in a relatively normal setup and slowly developing stranger events, until the twist reveals the secret at the end. Although pretty much everyone knows 'Jekyll and Hyde' as basically a metaphor for split personality, it is still a great read.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Discourses and Selected Writings

One of the most impressive books I've read, even though I'm not really a fan of non-fiction books to begin with. Epictetus' writing is an easy way to understand stoicism as he used his daily life to give examples on how to approach situations as a stoa, without dismissing God's presence. Highly recommended for those who wants to start learning about stoicism.

Rating: ★★★★★

Metamorphosis

My first Kafka and did leave quite the impression in me. Whether as a novella with deep relatable implications, or the complete opposite, a complete nonsense tale with no significant plot at the end. I was beyond fascinated after finishing this book, as the line between a disappointment—waste of time—, and an accomplishment—exploration and new experience— is blurred completely.

Rating: ★★★★☆
